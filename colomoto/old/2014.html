3rd CoLoMoTo meeting - Lausanne April 17-18 2014

Submitted by chaouiya on Sun, 02/23/2014 - 17:56
The third CoLoMoTo meeting took place in Lausanne at the Center for Integrative Genomics (CIG), organised by Aurélien Naldi, Claudine Chaouiya and Ioannis Xenarios.

Acknowledgements: SIB (Swiss Institute of Bioinformatics and SystemsX.ch (Swiss Research Initiative in Systems Biology)

Programme & participants
Meeting report
Back to CoLoMoTo home page

Progamme

 Thursday 17
  • 9:00-12:00 presentations (tools)
    • GINsim (A. Naldi)
    • BoolNet (C. Müssel & H. Kestler)
    • SQUAD (L. Mendoza)
    • the Cell Collective (T. Helikar)
    • EpiLog (P. Varela)
  • 14:00-15:00 presentations (tools continuation)
    • Attractors identification and quantification (P. Monteiro)
    • CellNOpt (T. Cokelaer)
  • 15:00 - 15:30 SBML qual package & SED-ML (C. Chaouiya, A. Nladi)
  • 15:30 - 16:00 JSBML (A. Dräger)
  • 16:00 discussion, working session
Friday 18
  • 9:00 - 12:00 Presentations (methods & models)
    • On the Generalization of Fixpoints of Boolean Networks (H. Klarner)
    • Model-checking applied to logical models (P. Monteiro)
    • A Discrete Model of Drosophila Eggshell Patterning (C. Chaouiya)
    • Continuous time modelling with MaBoSS. Application to bladder tumorigenesis (L. Calzone & G. Stoll)
  • 14:00 LogicalModel library, current status (A. Naldi)
  • 14:30 Discussion, CoLoMoTo future (community, scope, founding, etc.)
