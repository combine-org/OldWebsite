2nd CoLoMoTo meeting


This page is not in use any more. See the new CoLoMoTo site

Wellcome trust conference centre, Hinxton UK. Lunches will be served at Murrays, the Genome Campus restaurant. The meetings will be in the Pompeii room of the Conference Centre. Organizer: Systems Biomedicine group, EMBL-EBI
Cambridge, UK
Tuesday 27- Wednesday 28 March 2012

(This page is a copy of the information originally available at [1])

Agenda

Tuesday March 27

Presentations by Colomoto members, focusing on getting to know each other, and progress update

Time Topic Slides
13.00 Arrival, lunch at Murrays (the genome campus restaurant)
14.00 Opening, welcome
14.00-14.15 Julio Saez-Rodriguez: CellNOpt, modelling prior knowledge networks trained to data slides
14.20-14.35 Claudine Chaouiya: GINsim, current status and future developments slides
14.40-14.55 Denis Thieffry: Compression of state transitions graph for the analysis of cell fate decisions slides
15.00-15.15 Andreas Dräger: Modeling the influence of nuclear receptors and statins on hepatocyte metabolism slides
15.20-15.35 Tomas Helikar: The Cell Collective: Towards a more open, collaborative, and integrated systems biology. slides
15.35-16.00 Coffee break
16.00-16.15 Steffen Klamt: Qualitative modeling approaches for signaling and regulatory networks slides
Brainstorm on governance, funding slides
18.00 End of meeting
18.15 Bus to Cambridge
19.30 Conference dinner at The Anchor Pub
22.00 Taxi back to conference centre


Wednesday March 28

Developers presentations

Time Topic Slides
9.00 Colomoto toolbox, by Aurelien Naldi slides
9.30 SBML-qual validation workshop by Martijn van Iersel slides
10.00 SBML packaging workshop by Sarah Keating slides
10.30 Coffee break
11.00 jSBML workshop by Nicolas Rodriguez slides
11.30 brainstorm on development process, validation and interoperability slides
12.30 lunch
13.30 brainstorm on future development slides
14.30 Coffee break
15.00 End (with possibility for continuing discussion)
