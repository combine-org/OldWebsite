BioPAX
BioPAX is a standard language that aims to enable integration, exchange, visualization and analysis of biological pathway data. It is expressed in OWL.

The last specification of is BioPAX Level 3.

BioPAX is supported by many pathway database or processing tools.

BioPAX development is coordinated by an elected editorial board and a Scientific Advisory Board.
